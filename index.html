Hello there, I'm James Mejía

A lawyer heading to Frontend Development

Ever since I began my journey as a lawyer, I have always been drawn to the intersection of law and technology. As the digital landscape continues to transform our lives, I realized the immense potential of leveraging my legal background in combination with web development skills. The prospect of creating seamless and user-friendly online experiences through front-end web development ignited a passion within me. This motivation, coupled with a strong desire for continuous learning and growth, led me to embark on the path of transitioning from a successful legal career to becoming a front-end web developer.
